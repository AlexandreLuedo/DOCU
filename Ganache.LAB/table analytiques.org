** Guide to good conservation practices
Balance of balance of a chocolate ganache.
• Sugar: It is preferable to have a percentage of 25 to 30% of sugars (including that entering the composition of the coverage used.) For good conservation of the ganache.
• Cocoa butter: it is advisable to have 20% of the total weight of the recipe. Including that of the composition of the cover used. (For a covered ganache).
• Water: it is important to have a maximum percentage of water (milk, cream, butter, pulp ...) of 25% or minimum of dry extracts of 75% in order to have good conservation, otherwise we risk:
		○ unbalance the emulsion
		○ Promote bacteriological development
		○ Reduce the shelf life
